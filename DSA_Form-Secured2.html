Request for Accommodations

The University of Phoenix is committed to providing equal access to all courses, programs, services and activities. In accordance with federal law, we assist students with temporary or permanent medical conditions and other diagnoses that may qualify them for accommodations. You must fill out this form to request accommodations. While it is the first step, it connects you to a disability services advisor who will work with you to determine possible accommodations.
